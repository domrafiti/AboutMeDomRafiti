About Me

Hey There! My Name is Dominick Rafiti

Most people call me Dom. I am a Colorado Native and am living in Fort Collins with my Wife (Ashley) and our three boys (Avery, Wylie, and Sirus). Outside of family activities I enjoy running, learning to play the banjo, thinking, and photography.

Professionally, I have spent the last ten years in a highly regulated industry, working in roles with progressively more responsibility. In 2013 I returned to Regis University, as a non-traditional student, and graduated in 2019 with my Bachelor's in Business Administration with a focus in Project Management. Additionally, I have earned my Lean Six Sigma Green Belt.

For quite some time I have been interested in expanding my technical skillset and abilities and decided to use some of the forced downtime of the COVID-19 pandemic to purse a Software Development Coding Bootcamp from the University of Denver.
